# Privacy Policy - Biblical Heralds **Effective Date:** [06-05-2025] **Last Updated:** [06-05-2025] ## Our Commitment Biblical Heralds is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our biblical interpretation service. ## Information We Collect ### Information You Provide - **Account Information:** Name, email address (if you create an account) - **Donation Information:** Payment details processed securely through Stripe - **Feedback:** Comments, suggestions, and feature requests you submit - **Engagement Preferences:** Your chosen tier level (Passive, Participant, Productive, Partner) ### Information Automatically Collected - **Usage Data:** Which heralds you select, verses you explore, features you use - **Technical Information:** IP address, browser type, device information - **Analytics:** Page views, session duration, user interactions (anonymized) ### Information We Do NOT Collect - We do not track your personal Bible reading habits outside our service - We do not share your specific verse searches with third parties - We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary ## How We Use Your Information ### Primary Uses - **Service Delivery:** Provide biblical interpretations and Herald responses - **Communication:** Send newsletters and updates based on your engagement tier - **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance our service - **Support:** Respond to your questions and feedback ### Financial Operations - **Donations:** Process payments securely through Stripe - **Transparency:** Generate monthly funding reports (anonymized) - **Accounting:** Maintain records for financial transparency and tax purposes ## Information Sharing ### We Do NOT Sell Your Data Biblical Heralds will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. ### Limited Sharing We only share information in these specific circumstances: - **Payment Processing:** Stripe receives necessary payment information - **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our legal rights - **Service Providers:** Trusted partners who help operate our service (under strict confidentiality) ### Aggregated Data We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics about usage patterns to: - Demonstrate the impact of biblical education - Report to ministry partners and supporters - Improve biblical study resources ## Data Security ### Protection Measures - **Encryption:** All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption - **Secure Storage:** Data stored on secure, encrypted servers - **Limited Access:** Only authorized personnel can access personal information - **Regular Updates:** Security measures reviewed and updated regularly ### Payment Security - All payment information is processed by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor - We do not store credit card numbers or sensitive payment data - Stripe maintains the highest standards of payment security ## Your Rights and Choices ### Account Control - **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from emails at any time - **Tier Changes:** Modify your engagement level (Passive to Partner) - **Feedback Control:** Choose what feedback you provide ### Data Retention - **Active Accounts:** Retained as long as your account is active -
